Kolaborator Aktif

Industri & akademisi pada bidang manufaktur cerdas.

Kami memetakan agenda penelitian ke dalam tiga pilar utama untuk memastikan kesinambungan inovasi, keandalan solusi, serta kesiapan implementasi industri.

  • Integrated Manufacturing Intelligence

    Integrated product process modelling and simulations, manufacturing supply system monitoring and simulation, design and production optimization modelling, mathematical modelling, serta advanced production planning scheduling and inventory control.

  • Human-Centric Future Factory

    HMI / HRI, additive manufacturing, biomaterial, augmented reality / virtual reality system development, dan human automation based on bio-signal.

  • Strategic Operations & Quality Excellence

    Manufacturing systems, product design and development, production development system engineering, quality improvement, quality engineering and optimization, inventory modelling and management, operation research and management science, supply chain management, project management, serta smart facilities management.
